A network that offers DeFi investors a saferenvironment

Nothing speical

BerylBit is a fork of the ethereum network, which will be using the ethash PoW algorithm. Using high computing power will assure security on the network. The network supports EVM, allowing developers to build on the network using the solidity language and smart contracts. In the later stage, BerylBit plans to work towards providing private transactions for users.

Our DeFi network will host a unruggable exchange, where DeFi projects that pair liquidity to the native coin will not be able to pull liquidity under certain mechanisms. Curious how a UnRug dex will work? Read our Docs to find out more.

Liquidity pool looks Yum

BerylBit's main focus is the DeFi market. The aim is to provide a safer place for new investors and a less risky environment. Achieving this would mean lowering the price impact on the liquidity pool. We have planned a way to tackle this issue even with a decentralized network, the approach will be through a smart contract level. Tokens that pair through our DEX will have a less likely chance of pulling a rug due to our mechanism. The mechanism does support projects that need to pull their liquidity out, the UnRug DEX mechanism tries to aim towards scams only.
